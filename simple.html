A book is a medium for recording information in the form of writing or images. Modern books are typically in codex format.


Books are sold at both regular stores and specialized bookstores, as well as online for delivery, and can be borrowed from libraries.


As a conceptual object, a book refers to a written work of substantial length, which may be distributed either physically or digitally as an ebook.
